- COMPANY PROFILE -

From its modern showroom situated in a busy crossroads at the end of the Birkirkara Bypass in Malta, Bathroom Design has the displays and designs to satisfy all tastes and to suit all pockets.

Best known as the representatives of the prestigious German bathroom company Villeroy & Boch, Bathroom Design in Malta also represents a number of important names in the field of ceramic tiles. Fiandre are world leaders in porcelain stoneware and produce some of the most eye-catching tile designs ever created. Wall and floor tiles for domestic and commercial use are catered for by Panaria, Aparici, Zirconio, ABK and Alfa amongst others.

Bathroom Design now employs 40 staff divided between showroom, offices and state-of-the-art warehouse. The company's base is the 700sq m showroom on two floors with beautiful displays of the latest tile designs and the newest bathroom innovations. Customers paying a visit are guided by our trained sales personnel, assessing their requirements whilst offering design and practical advice on all aspect of bathroom design, to re-modelling, renovation and projects.

Bathroom Design's goal is to remain at the forefront of the bathroom supply sector in Malta and to keep on building on its reputation as a centre where customers can be assured of top-quality goods and the efficient service to match. With the help of thousands of satisfied patrons Bathroom Design will be here to ensure that fitting one's home will always be an agreeable and satisfying experience.

THE

START

1984

Bathroom Design was established back in 1984, importing and selling sanitary ware and ceramic tiles to the local market. In its early days, Bathroom Design stored its goods in a number of garages in the Iklin area and imported sanitary ware from several local and foreign suppliers. Since becoming the exclusive agents of Villeroy & Boch in 1985, the company has expanded exponentially, cultivating a reputation as one of the leading companies in Malta’s bathroom and sanitary ware sector.

- THE SHOWROOM -

The Bathroom Design showroom is strategically located in one of Malta’s busiest residential and commercial areas. The three-floor 6500sqm warehouse boasts the latest equipment to handle the large volumes of bathroom products, sanitary ware and tiles that are processed daily, and is invaluable part of the company’s success.

Bathroom Design inaugurated the new refurbished floor of their Birkirkara showroom with a launch event on the 25th of June. It is thanks to the continued backing of leading firms such as Villeroy & Boch that Bathroom Design has been able to maintain its steady business and strong reputation for three decades. The brand new refurbishment coincides with Bathroom Design’s 30th year in business as one of Malta’s leading bathroom suppliers.
